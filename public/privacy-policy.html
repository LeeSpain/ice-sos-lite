Privacy Policy - ICE SOS Lite

Effective Date: January 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide emergency services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, emergency contacts
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when emergency features are activated
  • Medical Information: Emergency medical details you provide
  • Device Information: Mobile device type, app usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide emergency assistance and contact emergency services
  • Share location and medical information with first responders
  • Send notifications to your emergency contacts
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We share information only when necessary:

  • Emergency Services: Location and medical data during emergencies
  • Emergency Contacts: Your status and location during emergencies
  • Service Providers: Secure cloud storage and analytics providers

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure data transmission, and access controls.

5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectify inaccurate information
  • Right to erase your data (with emergency service limitations)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6. Data Retention

We retain emergency data for legal and safety requirements. Non-emergency data is retained only as long as necessary.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 without parental consent.
